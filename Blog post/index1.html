I know its full form. It’s Least Common Multiple !

But do you know what is lcm and why it is called lcm?
lets explore!

Let’s first understand what multiple in LCM means.

MULTIPLE

A multiple is the result of multiplying a number by an integer.

let me explain multiple with an example. Let’s say we want to find a multiple of number 2. Then we will find the product of number 2 with integers and all the possible results that we will obtain are the multiple of 2.In this case some of the multiples of 2 are 2,4,6,8,10,12 …


The multiple of a number can be negative also! But in the case of LCM, we have to find out the positive multiples only.


Now we are ready to understand LCM once we know what multiple of a number is!

Let’s say for example we have to find the LCM of 2 numbers 3,7.

Step 1 : We will find some of the multiples of the given numbers.

Step 2 : We will select the least common multiple from the list.
